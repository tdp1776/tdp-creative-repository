# Books Map

This document tracks all book projects in the TDP Creative Repository.

---

## F-15
Type: Geopolitical / Military Thriller

Premise:
A U.S. Air Force serviceman with minimal intelligence knowledge is kidnapped by Iran. He represents the closest access they can obtain to actual U.S. intelligence, but he does not possess the information they believe he has. The story follows the psychological and strategic struggle between captor and captive.

Status: Early Concept

---

## Henry

Type: Sci-Fi / Technology Thriller

Premise:
Henry becomes involved with alien technology that manifests as a nanobot-based exoskin. The technology enhances physical capabilities and interacts with digital systems. A Korean female character learns to interpret digital communications and hack systems using the technology. A mysterious “non-man” figure eventually assists Henry while operating under his own internal conflict with superiors.

Status: Concept Development

## The Twelve

Type: Political Thriller

Premise:
A group forms with the intention of eliminating individuals they believe are corrupt or harmful to society. Members begin to question the morality of the operation after several actions are carried out, leading to internal conflict and increased risk of exposure.

Status: Developed Concept

## The Third Cross

Type: Biblical Historical Fiction

Premise:
A story centered on the repentant thief crucified beside Jesus. The narrative explores his life as a Judean bandit and culminates in the moment of redemption when he encounters Christ.

Status: Strong Concept
