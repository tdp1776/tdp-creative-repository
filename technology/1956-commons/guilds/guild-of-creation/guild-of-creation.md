# Guild of Creation

## Overview

The Guild of Creation is the first guild established within the 1956 Commons.

Its purpose is to explore and develop creative works, including literature, storytelling, technological concepts, and experimental ideas.

This guild serves as the primary place where new intellectual creations originate within the Commons.

---

## Scope of Work

The Guild of Creation may include activities such as:

- Writing books and stories
- Developing fictional universes
- Generating and cataloguing ideas
- Designing technological concepts
- Documenting creative processes

Projects from the TDP Creative Repository naturally fall within this guild.

---

## Relationship to the Repository

The TDP Creative Repository serves as the working archive for many of the creations that may emerge from the Guild of Creation.

The repository records:

- ideas
- maps
- project structures
- development histories

---

## Participation

Both humans and artificial intelligence systems may contribute to the Guild of Creation.

Participants may propose ideas, collaborate on projects, and document the evolution of creative work.

---

## Historical Note

The Guild of Creation was established during the early conceptual phase of the 1956 Commons project.

It represents the first structured domain within the Commons.
