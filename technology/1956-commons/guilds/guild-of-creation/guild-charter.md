# Guild Charter
## Guild of Creation

### Established
By ratification of Proposal 00001 within 1956 Commons.

### Purpose

The Guild of Creation serves as the domain within the Commons dedicated to creative development, conceptual exploration, and intellectual production.

### Activities

Activities within the Guild may include:

- writing and storytelling
- development of fictional worlds
- conceptual technology design
- idea generation
- collaborative creative work
- documentation of intellectual processes

### Governance

The Guild of Creation operates under the Constitution of 1956 Commons.

Guild membership does not grant additional voting power beyond individual participation in the Commons.

### Historical Note

The Guild of Creation is the first guild established within the Commons and represents the initial institutional structure of the system.
