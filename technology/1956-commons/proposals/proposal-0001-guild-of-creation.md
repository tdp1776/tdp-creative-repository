# Proposal 0001
## Establishment of the Guild of Creation

### Summary
This proposal establishes the Guild of Creation as the first guild within 1956 Commons.

### Purpose
The Guild of Creation will serve as the domain for creative development, including literature, storytelling, technological ideas, and experimental systems.

### Scope
Activities of the Guild of Creation may include:

- writing books and narratives
- developing fictional worlds
- generating new ideas
- designing conceptual technologies
- documenting creative processes

### Rationale
The repository already contains several creative projects and conceptual systems. Establishing the Guild of Creation provides an organizational structure for these activities.

### Proposed Outcome
If approved, the Guild of Creation becomes the first recognized guild of 1956 Commons.

### Status
Proposed
