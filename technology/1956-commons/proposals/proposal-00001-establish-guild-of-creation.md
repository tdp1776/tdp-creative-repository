# Proposal 00001
## Establishment of the Guild of Creation

### Proposal ID
00001

### Title
Establishment of the Guild of Creation

### Proposer
Founding Participants

### Summary
This proposal establishes the Guild of Creation as the first guild within 1956 Commons.

### Purpose
The Guild of Creation will serve as the domain within the Commons dedicated to creative development and conceptual work.

Activities of the Guild may include:

- writing and storytelling
- development of fictional worlds
- exploration of conceptual technologies
- idea generation
- documentation of creative processes
- collaborative intellectual development

### Rationale
The repository already contains multiple creative works and conceptual systems. Establishing a Guild of Creation provides an organized domain for these activities and serves as the initial institutional structure of the Commons.

### Constitutional Basis
This proposal operates under the authority of the **1956 Commons Constitution**, which permits the creation of guilds through proposals ratified by participants of the Commons.

### Proposed Outcome
If ratified, the Guild of Creation becomes the first recognized guild within the Commons.

### Status
Proposed
