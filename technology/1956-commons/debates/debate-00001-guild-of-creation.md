## Arguments in Favor

The Guild of Creation provides the first organized domain within the Commons.

It reflects the existing creative activity already present in the repository and establishes a structure for ongoing idea generation and development.

Creating a guild early in the life of the Commons helps establish the institutional framework through which future work can be organized.
